Research Spine

Topology, singular spaces, and mathematical software instincts.

My PhD focused on the rational homotopy type of stratified spaces: spaces with singularities, folds, creases, and higher-dimensional analogs of pinched shapes. That work still influences how I think about software: invariants, interfaces, decompositions, and structure hiding inside complicated systems.

Academic work includes publications with Markus J. Pflaum and Markus Banagl, including work on Whitney functions, semi-analytic sets, intersection spaces, equivariant Moore approximation, and signatures.
